Let's get our hands dirty

Welcome to our exciting seminar on soil health!
Whether you're a passionate hobby gardener or a small-scale farmer, join us for an enriching exploration of the very foundation of successful cultivation—soil. Get ready for a hands-on experience as we dig into the earth, allowing you to touch and feel the soil, gaining a deeper understanding of its composition and structure. Our seminar goes beyond the surface as we employ simple equipment to analyze the soil, discussing the results and delving into eco-friendly tilling methods that promote sustainable and fruitful growth.

The seminar is an all-day event, providing ample time to engage with soil and learn valuable insights. Guests are welcome to join us for a healthy vegetarian lunch served in our roomy greenhouse—a perfect opportunity for networking and further discussions on soil health and sustainable farming practices. Come and unearth the secrets beneath your feet, and cultivate a deeper appreciation for the life-sustaining essence of healthy soil.

What we'll talk about

Soil composition

  • Understanding the Components: Learn about the key constituents that make up soil, including minerals, organic matter, water, and air.
  • Soil Texture: Delve into the different soil textures—sandy, loamy, and clayey—and their implications for plant growth and water retention.
  • Soil Structure: Explore how soil particles arrange themselves into aggregates, affecting porosity and root penetration.
  • Soil Formation: Discuss the processes involved in soil formation, including weathering, erosion, deposition, and biological activities that shape the characteristics of soil.
  • Role of Organic Material: Delve into the significance of organic material, such as decomposed plant and animal matter, in soil formation and its impact on soil structure, fertility, and sustainability.
